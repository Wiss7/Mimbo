How To Use:

- Whistles have been used for dog training and communication for generations.

- You can use a whistle to talk with your dog and establish a behavioral routine.

- Don't use the whistle close to their ears. Dogs hear at a much higher frequency than humans, so they're naturally more sensitive to sounds.

- If your dog responds to a verbal command, issue the command and then start the whistle for a couple of seconds. Reward your dog for responding and then carry on with the training.

- Eventually, the dog will associate the whistle blast with the command, and the whistle will be all you need.
