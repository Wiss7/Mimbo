Dog Clicker

How To Use:

- To use a clicker, you’ll first need to teach the dog what the clicker means.

- To teach a dog the meaning of the click, a treat is given immediately after clicking.

- After about 10–20 repetitions, your dog will understand that the marker predicts a coming reward.

- You can test your success by clicking when your dog is not paying attention to you. If your dog responds to the click by suddenly looking at you, then looking for a treat, you are ready to move on.

- Teach your dog commands using the clicker. At the exact moment your dog performs the desired action, press the clicker. Follow with a treat and praise.
